Freeze Ray (Power-Up)

In Minion Rush, the Freeze Ray power-up allows you to freeze obstacles, and once you hit a frozen obstacle, it will get smashed and destroyed. In older versions of the game, it was possible to upgrade the Freeze Ray power-up's duration using bananas and tokens. However, as of the 5 Year Celebration update, the power-up's duration depends on the costume you are using.
