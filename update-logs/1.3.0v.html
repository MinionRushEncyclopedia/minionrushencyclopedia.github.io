Version 1.3.0

Version 1.3.0 introduced a brand new variant for the "Residential Area" location (this variant is known as the "Halloween Residential Area"), a brand new variant of the Meena boss battle, brand new obstacle designs, and 4 new costumes (Knight, Partier, Vampire, and Hunter). This update released for the App Store on October 8, 2013.
