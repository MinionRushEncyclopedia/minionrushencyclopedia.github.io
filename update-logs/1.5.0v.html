Version 1.5.0

Version 1.5.0 introduced a brand new variant for the "Gru's Lab" location (this variant is known as the "Holiday Lab"). Furthermore, it also introduced a festive Vector boss battle, brand new obstacle designs, and 2 new costumes (Santa and Snowboarder).
