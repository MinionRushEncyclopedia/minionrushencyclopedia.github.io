Lifeguard Costume

Added in the Exclusive 2.4.1 iOS version, The Lifeguard (AKA (LIFEGUARD) RED) Costume is a playable character in Minion Rush. Currently, the costume costs 50 Lifeguard Costume Cards and 10 Banana Coins. The costume was once a symbol for RED fighting against Aids, but is now a regularly obtained outfit.
