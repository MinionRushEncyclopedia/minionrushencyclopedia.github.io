Astronaut Costume

The Astronaut Costume is an "Epic" costume that was released in version 1.7.1, and its initial cost was $19.99US. In some cases, the costume could be found for a token price (usually 2,200 tokens). After the 5 Year Celebration update, the costume could be obtained for 35 Astronaut Costume Cards and 10 Banana Coins.
