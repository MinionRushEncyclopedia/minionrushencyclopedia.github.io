Baker Costume

The Baker Costume is a playable character in Minion Rush, and it was released in version 1.4.0. This costume can be unlocked with 60 Baker Costume Cards and 10 Banana Coins. Furthermore, the costume’s ability is “Get 20% more Despicable Actions for missions that require them”, its class is “common”, and its power-up is the XXL Minion.
