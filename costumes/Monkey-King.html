Monkey King Costume

The Monkey King Costume is a playable character in Chinese Android variants of Minion Rush, and it was released in version 4.2.0k. Currently, the costume costs 50 Monkey King Costume Cards and 10 Banana Coins. However, the version he was avaliable in was taken offline and reverted to the Jelly Lab, where he costs 20 Yuan.
